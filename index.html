Services

Cyber Forensics

Cyber forensics is the process of finding and studying digital evidence. It helps to solve cyber crimes like hacking, fraud, and data theft. Experts collect data from computers, mobiles, and networks. This evidence is used to understand what happened and who did it.

Ethical Hacking

Ethical hacking means finding security problems in computer systems legally. Ethical hackers test websites, networks, and apps to find weaknesses. They help companies fix issues before real hackers attack. This makes systems safer and more secure.

Bug Bounty

Bug bounty is a program where companies reward people for finding security bugs. Ethical hackers test websites and applications for vulnerabilities. They report the bugs instead of misusing them. In return, companies pay money or give rewards.
